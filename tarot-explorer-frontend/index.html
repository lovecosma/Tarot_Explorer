Welcome to the Tarot Spread Explorer

Use this application to choose a spread type and generate three Tarot cards to fill that spread.

First choose the type of spread you would like to draw below. You should clearly envision a question in your mind for which the spread can help you find the answer.

Then explore the relationships between your query, the cards' meanings, and their spots in the spread.

Which problem's persistently plague people? You can view details from pervious queries below. All queries are anonymous.

*Remember*

Cards have different meanings depending on whether or not they are upright or in reverse.
